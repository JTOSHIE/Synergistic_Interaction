## Purpose

messaging without naming the client. The five failure vectors are mapped to the nine
components to demonstrate what happens when compliance-first category management is absent.

## Approved Language Patterns

These patterns ensure impact without attribution:

| ❌ NEVER USE | ✅ USE INSTEAD |
|-------------|---------------|
| "A major retailer we worked with..." | "Retailers operating without compliance infrastructure..." |
| "In a recent engagement..." | "Category managers who build compliance-first..." |
| "Our client experienced..." | "The cost of compliance failure in retail..." |
| Any specific dollar figure from the engagement | Industry benchmark figures |
| Specific regulatory breach names | General regulatory categories (ACCC, CAV) |
