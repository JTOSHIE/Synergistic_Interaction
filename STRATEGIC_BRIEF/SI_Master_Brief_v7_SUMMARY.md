## Core Strategic Themes

### Theme 1: Compliance as Competitive Advantage
Synergistic Interaction's differentiation is the reframing of compliance from cost burden to growth enabler. The "licence to go faster" concept is central to all messaging.

### Theme 2: Nine-Component Architecture as Intellectual Property
The nine-component framework (with ISO 37301 alignment) is the consultancy's primary IP and must be communicated as proprietary methodology — not generic best practice.

### Theme 3: Implicit Retrospective Positioning
The Panda Mart engagement failure (as a past case) is woven implicitly into all component descriptions to demonstrate the cost of non-compliance without naming the client.

### Theme 4: Dual-Market Authority
The website must simultaneously establish credibility with Australian retail (English) and Chinese retail (Mandarin transcreation) audiences.
